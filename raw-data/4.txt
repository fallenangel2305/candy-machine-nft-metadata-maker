Torro I

A planet-wide ocean mostly free of ice, except at the poles.  

Large yellow creatures use sails to drift across the ocean surface. They are able to emit laser-like blasts of light to hurt and disorient their enemies.

The planet is perfectly round, with no equatorial bulge.

Habitability: 40%
Size: 3
Industry: 2
Science: 6
